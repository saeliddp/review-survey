Instructions

1. You will be shown a description of a recommendation and three explanations for that recommendation. Your task is to rate each of them for their quality on a scale from 1 to 5, with 1 having lowest quality and 5 having highest quality. Note that all of them can be good and all of them can be bad. In other words, one explanation’s quality doesn’t affect the others.

2. Click submit to confirm your rating.

3. You will then be directed to a new page to complete the next task.

4. There are 10 tasks in total. After you have submitted all, we will also ask you to briefly explain how you made your decisions.

5. At the end, enter your MTurk ID and obtain a code that you will need to enter in MTurk HIT to be verified and paid.
